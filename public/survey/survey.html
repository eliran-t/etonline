The Survey.

Data collection:

This survey is designed to help pinpointing workflows most suitable for optimization. We look for:
  • Tasks that are time-consuming, repetitive, error-prone or even just plain annoying.
  • Areas where improved data access enables quicker, more informed decisions (e.g. dashboards / notifications/ reports).

Solution Proposal:

based on the survey outcome we'll build a plan to streamline your operations and reveal growth opportunities, but most importantly, empower your team to focus on tasks they are passionate about and play to their strengths.

The Toolbox.

Automation:

Streamline repetitive tasks without manual effort, such as:
  • Event-based actions: Send follow-up emails to customers after a purchase or abandoned cart.
  • Time-driven tasks: Generate weekly sales reports, or schedule social media posts in advance.
  • Workflow management: Streamline the approval process for invoices or expense reports.
  • Threshold-based alerts:low inventory alert, delayed shipment notification.
  • Data processing: Generate sales dashboards, customer retention reports.

AI:

Perform tasks that typically require human-like intelligence, such as:
  • Analyzing data: Analyze customer feedback to identify trends and areas for improvement.
  • Identifying patterns: Forecast sales trends to optimize inventory levels and avoid stock-outs.
  • Customer interaction: Provide customer support and personalized product recommendations through AI-powered chatbots.
